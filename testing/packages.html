FAQ

Everything you need to know was clearly answered.

How do I book a tour?
+
You can easily book a tour by browsing our packages section, selecting your desired destination, and clicking "Booking Open" or "View Details". Fill out the contact form and our travel experts will get in touch with you shortly.
Is my payment secure?
+
Yes! We use industry-standard encryption to protect your payment details. Transactions are securely processed through trusted third-party payment gateways.
What's your cancellation policy?
+
Our cancellation policy varies depending on the package and airline partner rules. Generally, cancellations made 30 days prior to the trip are eligible for a partial refund.
Do you provide international tours?
+
Absolutely! Aside from the beautiful domestic destinations in the Philippines, we also offer curated packages for Asia, Europe, and other international hotspots.
